INTJ – The Architect

The INTJ personality type is known as the Architect – a strategic, imaginative thinker with a plan for everything. These individuals are confident, analytical, and love diving deep into complex systems to solve problems efficiently.

INTJs often value intelligence, independence, and competence. They can sometimes come across as blunt or perfectionistic, but their core motivation is often to improve systems and help others grow.

Their key cognitive functions are: Introverted Intuition (Ni), Extraverted Thinking (Te), Introverted Feeling (Fi), and Extraverted Sensing (Se).
