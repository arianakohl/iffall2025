ESFP – The Entertainer

ESFPs are outgoing, vibrant, and full of life. As Entertainers, they light up any room with energy and humor, and they love being at the center of the action.

These individuals live for the present moment, embracing experiences and people with open arms. ESFPs are spontaneous, sociable, and eager to make life a fun, meaningful adventure for everyone around them.

Core cognitive functions include: Extraverted Sensing (Se), Introverted Feeling (Fi), Extraverted Thinking (Te), and Introverted Intuition (Ni).
