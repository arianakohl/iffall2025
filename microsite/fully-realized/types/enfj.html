ENFJ – The Protagonist

ENFJs are charismatic and inspiring leaders, driven by a deep sense of empathy and a passion for helping others grow. As Protagonists, they believe in doing the right thing and rallying people toward shared goals and values.

Warm and expressive, ENFJs build strong connections with others and often take on mentorship or guiding roles. They excel in communication and are motivated by a vision of a better, more harmonious world.

Core cognitive functions include: Extraverted Feeling (Fe), Introverted Intuition (Ni), Extraverted Sensing (Se), and Introverted Thinking (Ti).
