ESTP – The Entrepreneur

ESTPs are energetic, perceptive, and action-oriented. As Entrepreneurs, they live in the moment and thrive on excitement, often seeking out new challenges and fast-paced environments.

They are social, bold, and persuasive — ready to take risks and improvise to achieve their goals. ESTPs are known for their sharp observation skills and ability to read people and situations quickly.

Core cognitive functions include: Extraverted Sensing (Se), Introverted Thinking (Ti), Extraverted Feeling (Fe), and Introverted Intuition (Ni).
