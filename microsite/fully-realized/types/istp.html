ISTP – The Virtuoso

ISTPs are bold experimenters and independent problem-solvers. As Virtuosos, they love figuring out how things work and enjoy hands-on activities where they can apply practical logic and technical skill.

They’re adaptable, action-oriented, and often excel in situations that require quick thinking and physical coordination. ISTPs enjoy exploring the world through their senses and mastering new tools or systems.

Core cognitive functions include: Introverted Sensing (Si), Extraverted Thinking (Te), Introverted Feeling (Fi), and Extraverted Intuition (Ne).
