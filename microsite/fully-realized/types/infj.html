INFJ – The Advocate

The INFJ personality type is known as the Advocate – a quiet, insightful individual with a deep sense of purpose. They are driven by a desire to help others and improve the world in meaningful ways.

Often considered one of the rarest types, INFJs combine vision and empathy to create positive change. They are idealistic but organized, guided by strong values and a deep inner drive.

Core cognitive functions include: Introverted Intuition (Ni), Extraverted Feeling (Fe), Introverted Thinking (Ti), and Extraverted Sensing (Se).
