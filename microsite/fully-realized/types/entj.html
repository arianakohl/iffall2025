ENTJ – The Commander

ENTJs are natural-born leaders, driven by a desire to lead others to success through clear strategy and execution. Known as Commanders, they thrive on challenge and value efficiency, structure, and big-picture thinking.

With bold vision and unwavering determination, ENTJs often rise to leadership roles and excel at organizing teams, systems, and ideas to achieve ambitious goals.

Core cognitive functions include: Extraverted Thinking (Te), Introverted Intuition (Ni), Extraverted Sensing (Se), and Introverted Feeling (Fi).
