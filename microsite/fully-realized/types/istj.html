ISTJ – The Logistician

ISTJs are responsible, dependable, and grounded in practicality. Known as Logisticians, they take pride in doing things by the book and value honesty, order, and hard work.

They often form the backbone of organizations, making sure processes run smoothly and tasks get done properly. ISTJs thrive on structure and believe in doing their duty with quiet integrity.

Core cognitive functions include: Introverted Sensing (Si), Extraverted Thinking (Te), Introverted Feeling (Fi), and Extraverted Intuition (Ne).
