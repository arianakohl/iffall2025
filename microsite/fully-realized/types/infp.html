INFP – The Mediator

INFPs are quiet idealists, guided by their core values and beliefs. As Mediators, they are deeply empathetic, imaginative, and driven by a sense of moral purpose and meaning.

Though they may seem reserved, INFPs possess rich inner lives and a strong desire to understand themselves and others. They are passionate about helping people and often express their values through art, writing, or activism.

Core cognitive functions include: Introverted Feeling (Fi), Extraverted Intuition (Ne), Introverted Sensing (Si), and Extraverted Thinking (Te).
