ISFJ – The Defender

ISFJs are nurturing, dependable, and deeply loyal. As Defenders, they combine kindness and responsibility, often working behind the scenes to protect and care for the people they love.

They have a strong sense of duty and tradition, and they often go out of their way to preserve harmony and support others. Though quiet, ISFJs can be incredibly strong in their convictions and actions.

Core cognitive functions include: Introverted Sensing (Si), Extraverted Feeling (Fe), Introverted Thinking (Ti), and Extraverted Intuition (Ne).
