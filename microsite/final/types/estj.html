ESTJ – The Executive

ESTJs are organized, practical, and committed to duty. As Executives, they value structure and thrive in leadership roles where they can implement procedures and uphold traditions.

They are strong-willed, loyal, and often serve as pillars of their communities and institutions. ESTJs take pride in bringing people together to accomplish tasks efficiently and correctly.

Core cognitive functions include: Extraverted Thinking (Te), Introverted Sensing (Si), Extraverted Intuition (Ne), and Introverted Feeling (Fi).
