ENFP – The Campaigner

ENFPs are free spirits—creative, outgoing, and energetic. As Campaigners, they love exploring new ideas, meeting new people, and expressing themselves in unique ways. They bring enthusiasm and optimism into everything they do.

With a natural ability to see potential, ENFPs often inspire those around them to dream bigger. They thrive in dynamic environments and are drawn to causes that bring meaning and change.

Core cognitive functions include: Extraverted Intuition (Ne), Introverted Feeling (Fi), Extraverted Thinking (Te), and Introverted Sensing (Si).
