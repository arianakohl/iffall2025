ENTP – The Debater

ENTPs are bold, curious, and full of energy. Known as the Debater, this personality type thrives on mental sparring, innovation, and new ideas. They are natural brainstormers and love to explore every possible angle.

They’re not necessarily looking for conflict — rather, they enjoy a good challenge and love refining their thoughts by engaging in discussion. ENTPs are masters of improvisation and value flexibility over structure.

Core cognitive functions include: Extraverted Intuition (Ne), Introverted Thinking (Ti), Extraverted Feeling (Fe), and Introverted Sensing (Si).
