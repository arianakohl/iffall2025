ISFP – The Adventurer

ISFPs are gentle, flexible, and creative individuals. Known as Adventurers, they explore life through rich sensory experiences and express themselves in unique, artistic ways.

They often have a quiet, curious nature, and they appreciate beauty, nature, and meaningful connections. ISFPs tend to follow their hearts and value authenticity over tradition or convention.

Core cognitive functions include: Introverted Feeling (Fi), Extraverted Sensing (Se), Introverted Intuition (Ni), and Extraverted Thinking (Te).
