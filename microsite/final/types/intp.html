INTP – The Logician

INTPs are innovative, curious, and deeply analytical. Known as the Logician, they are often seen lost in thought, exploring theoretical frameworks and abstract ideas with precision and depth.

They seek truth and logical clarity above all, often coming up with inventive solutions and novel perspectives. INTPs are independent thinkers who enjoy mental challenges and deep problem-solving.

Core cognitive functions include: Introverted Thinking (Ti), Extraverted Intuition (Ne), Introverted Sensing (Si), and Extraverted Feeling (Fe).
