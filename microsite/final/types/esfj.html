ESFJ – The Consul

ESFJs are warm, sociable, and dedicated to helping others. Known as Consuls, they are highly attuned to the needs of those around them and thrive in environments where cooperation and harmony are valued.

They are strong organizers and often take on community-centered roles, building supportive networks and ensuring people feel appreciated and understood.

Core cognitive functions include: Extraverted Feeling (Fe), Introverted Sensing (Si), Extraverted Intuition (Ne), and Introverted Thinking (Ti).
